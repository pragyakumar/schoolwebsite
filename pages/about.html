About us

Welcome To
Montessori School

On behalf of our school community, we welcome you to Armadale Primary School where we all work together to develop in our students the capacity to become independent life-long learners and to equip them with the skills and knowledge to pursue their goals. An outstanding feature of the school continues to be the very strong sense of community that prevails. Teachers and parents share a joint responsibility for helping each child develop his or her talents and abilities and consequently strong partnerships are forged between the home and school environments. Parental involvement in all facets of the school is always welcomed and valued.
